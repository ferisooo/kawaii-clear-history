# Privacy Policy — Kawaii Cleaner

**Last updated:** 06/21/2026 (L.A. time)

---

## TL;DR (the short version)

* **Feris does not collect anything.** No personal data, no tracking, no
  accounts, no servers.
* Everything the App does happens **on your own device.**
* The App **never sends your data anywhere.**

---

## 1. We do not collect your data

Feris Imagination, the maker of Kawaii Cleaner, **does not collect, store,
receive, sell, share, or transmit any information** from people who use the App.
There are no analytics, no trackers, no advertising, no user accounts, and no
servers operated by us that receive your data.

## 2. Everything happens on your device

The App works entirely on your own computer. When you clear browsing data, the
App asks your browser to delete that data locally. We never see it, and it is
never uploaded to us or anyone else.

## 3. We can't see what you delete

We have no way of knowing what you clear, when you use the App, or what is on
your device. Because nothing is collected, there is nothing for us to look at,
leak, or hand over.

## 4. Browser permissions

The App uses a single browser permission, **`browsingData`**, for one reason
only: to let your browser erase the data you choose to clear. This permission is
used locally and is never used to read, copy, or send your data to us.

## 5. Links to Google's pages

Some buttons open Google's own pages (such as My Activity) in a new tab so you
can manage your account history there. Once you're on a Google page, **Google's**
privacy policy applies — not ours. We don't receive anything from those pages
and have no control over them.

## 6. No cookies or accounts

The App does not create an account, does not require sign-in, and does not set
its own cookies or identifiers to track you.

## 7. Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children, because it does not collect information at all.

## 8. Forks and modified versions

If someone forks or modifies the App, **this policy covers only the original
version distributed by Feris Imagination.** We are not responsible for the
privacy practices of any modified or third-party version. Always check the
privacy policy of the version you actually install.

## 9. Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will change too.
Since the App collects nothing, any update will simply clarify that fact.

## 10. Contact

Questions about privacy can be directed to the project maintainer through the
project's repository.
